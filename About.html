I'm Malcome Lismore

Photographer | Traveller

"Photography is a way of feeling, of touching, of loving. What you have caught on film is captured forever ... It remembers little things, long after you have forgotten everything." -Aaron Siskind

Who am I....

I am Malcolm Lismore, a dedicated freelance photographer rooted in the breathtaking landscapes of the North West coast of Scotland. Captivated by the raw beauty of nature, I have made it my mission to freeze these moments in time through my lens. Beyond the lens, I am an individual driven by a profound connection to the environment that surrounds me. My journey in photography has been an odyssey of discovery, where each click of the shutter has unveiled new facets of the world around me. From the rugged Scottish terrain to the delicate coastal birds, I have endeavored to encapsulate the essence of these scenes, offering a visual narrative that speaks to the heart.

My Experience......

With years of honing my craft, my photography is a testament to the evolution of skills and an unyielding commitment to the art form. I have traversed the diverse landscapes of Scotland, absorbing the nuances of its changing seasons and the myriad creatures that call it home. My expertise extends beyond the realms of nature photography. Weddings, portraits, and special events have provided me with opportunities to diversify my portfolio. Each assignment is an invitation to weave visual tales, capturing the emotions and memories that define these special moments. I am not merely a photographer; I am a storyteller. My lens is a conduit through which stories unfold, whether it be the majestic vistas of the Scottish highlands or the intimate moments shared between loved ones on their special day.

My Passion.....

Photography is more than a profession for me; it is a profound passion that fuels my creativity and drives me to explore the boundaries of visual storytelling. The natural world, with its untamed beauty, is my primary muse, inspiring a relentless pursuit of the perfect shot. The challenge of encapsulating the fleeting beauty of wildlife, the play of light on landscapes, and the intimate moments in human connections is what fuels my passion. Through my lens, I aim not just to document scenes but to evoke emotions and stir a sense of wonder in those who view my work. In every photograph, I aim to share the love and fascination I feel for the world around me. My passion for photography is an ongoing journey, a continuous exploration of the ever-changing tapestry of life that I am fortunate to witness and capture.
